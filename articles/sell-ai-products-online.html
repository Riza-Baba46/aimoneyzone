How to Sell AI-Generated Products Online (2025)

Artificial intelligence has opened new opportunities for creators to make and sell products online with minimal effort. Whether you want to sell eBooks, artwork, templates, or digital tools — AI makes it faster than ever.

1. Choose What to Sell

Popular AI-generated products include:

  • eBooks written with ChatGPT
  • Wall art or posters made with Midjourney
  • Notion templates or digital planners
  • Merch designs for print-on-demand stores

2. Generate Your Product with AI

Use tools like:

  • ChatGPT: for writing books, guides, or product descriptions
  • Midjourney: for visual art and illustrations
  • Canva: for refining designs

3. Choose a Platform to Sell

Here are top platforms to sell AI products:

  • Gumroad: for digital downloads
  • Etsy: for printable wall art, templates
  • Amazon KDP: for eBooks
  • Redbubble / Teespring: for print-on-demand

4. Automate Delivery

Use automation tools like Zapier or Gumroad’s built-in email system to deliver products instantly and manage orders hands-free.

5. Promote Your Store

Leverage social media, SEO, or AI-generated content (blogs, videos) to drive traffic to your shop. The more visibility, the more passive income you generate.
