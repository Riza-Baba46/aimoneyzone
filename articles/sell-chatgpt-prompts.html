How to Sell ChatGPT Prompts and Make Money (2025)

Prompt engineering has become one of the most underrated ways to make money online using AI. Instead of writing content or building tools, you simply create powerful prompts—and sell them. Here's how to get started in 2025.

What Is a Prompt?

A prompt is the text instruction you give to ChatGPT to produce a specific output. Great prompts can generate high-quality copy, ideas, code, and much more. These are valuable assets!

Where to Sell Prompts

  • PromptBase: One of the top marketplaces dedicated to prompt selling.
  • Gumroad: Create a digital product and sell prompt packs.
  • Etsy: Yes, even Etsy allows AI-related digital products now.

Best Practices

  • Focus on a niche (e.g., marketing prompts, resume writing, business plans)
  • Test all prompts to ensure they work consistently
  • Bundle prompts into themed packs for more value
  • Write clear titles and descriptions (SEO helps even on Gumroad!)

How Much Can You Earn?

Some creators make $500+ per month passively. While results vary, a few high-quality prompt packs can generate recurring income with almost zero maintenance.
