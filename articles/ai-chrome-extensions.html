Best AI Chrome Extensions for Productivity (2025)

AI isn't just for big tech anymore—now it's in your browser. These Chrome extensions use artificial intelligence to save time, boost creativity, and help you work smarter in 2025.

1. GrammarlyGO

Beyond grammar correction, GrammarlyGO helps you write better and faster with AI-generated suggestions, email drafts, and even tone adjustment.

2. Compose AI

A free Chrome extension that uses GPT to auto-complete your writing anywhere—Gmail, Google Docs, and more.

3. Glasp

Save, summarize, and share highlights from any webpage using AI. Perfect for researchers, bloggers, and productivity nerds.

4. Merlin

Turn ChatGPT into your sidekick. Merlin lets you use GPT anywhere online—LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube comments, Google searches, etc.

5. Wordtune

A rewriting tool powered by AI to make your sentences sound clearer, smarter, or more professional in one click.

6. Perplexity AI

A search engine AI built into Chrome—get AI-powered answers instantly, right from the toolbar.
